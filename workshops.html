Workshops and Speaking Engagements

I am available to present workshops and speaking engagements for groups and organizations on issues that impact student well-being across different age groups. The content of presentations is grounded in research-based psychological knowledge and blended with anecdotal experiences to make it accessible and applicable to everyday practice.

Sample audiences and topics:

• Parents: general parenting guidance (e.g., limit-setting), school choice, managing strong emotions, peer relationships (friendships, meanness, bullying)
• Educators: classroom management, de-escalation strategies, managing peer conflict, addressing meanness and bullying, and having difficult conversations with parents
• Student-Athletes: dealing with stress and anxiety, relationships with coaches and teammates, time-management, self-evaluation, performance expectations, and response to feedback.
